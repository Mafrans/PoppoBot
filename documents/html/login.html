PoppoBot - The inferior Bot

PoppoBot is the lovechild of Mafrans's programming skills and spare time, and while she is not the best of bots she is regularly updated and just straight up cute.

PoppoBot was created for the purpose of being both an utility bot and an entertainment bot. While not being the first of her kind, neither from the side of her creators accomplishments, she is currently his most refined work with interesting features. Both her adorable name and overall cute aesthetic prove to be extremely pleasing to those who decide to use the bot.
